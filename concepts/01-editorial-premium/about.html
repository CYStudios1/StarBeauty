Our story

One store. One idea. Twenty-nine years.

Star Beauty opened its first doors on Ashland Avenue in 1995. Not because the neighborhood needed another beauty supply store — there were plenty. Because it needed a better one.

The shelves were stocked by people who used what they sold. The prices were honest. The advice was free. Word traveled fast. One store became two. Two became seven. And for twenty-nine years, the standard hasn’t changed.

Every Star Beauty location is family-operated. The buyers know the brands because they wear the brands. When a new texture product hits, it’s in our stores before it’s trending. That’s not luck — it’s relationships built over three decades with the same suppliers, the same neighborhoods, the same women who walk through our doors every Saturday morning before anyone else opens.

We know what 4C hair needs in February. We know which edge control actually holds through a Chicago August. We know because we live it — same climate, same water, same wind off the lake. This isn’t research. It’s life.

What we believe

Three rules. No exceptions.

01

Stock what works.

We don’t chase trends. We test everything on ourselves, our families, our neighbors. If it doesn’t work on real hair, it doesn’t make our shelves. Every product in a Star Beauty store has been worn by someone who works there. That’s the filter — not a spreadsheet, not a sales rep’s pitch.

02

Price it right.

Premium doesn’t mean expensive. We negotiate directly with suppliers so you get salon-quality products at neighborhood prices. Braids from $2.99. Human hair bundles from $64.99. We’ve never believed that good hair care should cost more on the South Side than it does anywhere else.

03

Know your name.

Seven stores, and the woman behind every counter remembers what you bought last time. That’s not a database — that’s twenty-nine years of showing up. When your stylist cancels and you need a quick twist-out for a funeral tomorrow, we’re the call you make. And we pick up.
